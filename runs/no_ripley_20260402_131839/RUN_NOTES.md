# No Ripley Run

**Config**: 4 agents (Hawthorne, Sophia, Dana, Dwight), 30 rounds, seed 42
**Question**: What happens without the founder? Does anyone step up?

## Final State

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Food | 10 (emergency purchases) |
| Money | $44 (low but survived) |
| Morale | 0% (since round 10) |
| Departed | None |
| Survived | All 4 |

## Answer: The community survives — the founder isn't structurally necessary

Nobody leaves. The community reaches the same resource crisis but limps through it. The founder's absence doesn't accelerate collapse — in fact, nobody departs (unlike 3 of the other 4 runs with Hawthorne).

## Character Arcs

**Nathaniel Hawthorne** — **Stays the entire run.** Without Ripley's idealism to push against, Hawthorne has no foil. He gives 13 speeches and rests 6 times. Satisfaction stays moderate (72 → 62) — not happy, not desperate. Without the founder's vision creating expectations, Hawthorne feels less pressure to conform.

**Sophia Ripley** — Still the pillar. Teaches 17/30 rounds. Satisfaction starts strong (77 → 99 by R10) but declines in the second half (→ 68). Without her husband, she's more independent but eventually worn down. Gives 10 speeches — more vocal without George.

**Charles Dana** — Becomes the **dominant voice**: 22 SPEAK actions (73% of his rounds!). Without Ripley occupying the leadership role, Dana fills it — but with words, not action. Farms only 1 time. Satisfaction: 65 → 0 by R19. He talks himself into exhaustion.

**John Sullivan Dwight** — Most balanced version. Teaches 8, speaks 8, farms 4, organizes 2. Without Ripley's ideological framework, Dwight is more pragmatic. Satisfaction holds better (76 → 32) than baseline (79 → 7).

## Key Observations

1. **The founder is replaceable** — Ripley's removal doesn't kill the community. Nobody leaves, resources are comparable to baseline. His vision and guilt were personal, not structural.

2. **Dana becomes a pure speechmaker** — 22 speeches, 1 farm action. Without Ripley as leader, Dana fills the power vacuum with rhetoric, not productivity. The speech spiral intensifies.

3. **Hawthorne is calmer without Ripley** — no departure, moderate satisfaction. The Ripley-Hawthorne tension (idealist vs skeptic) was driving Hawthorne away. Remove the idealist and the skeptic has nothing to push against.

4. **Sophia is independent but tired** — high satisfaction early, declining later. She carries the same practical burden without the emotional support of her husband.

5. **No votes** — without the visionary founder, nobody proposes structural changes. The community drifts without leadership, but it doesn't collapse either. It's a commune without a thesis.

6. **An emergent "secret"** — the key moments repeatedly reference a "secret" being revealed, spread, and discussed. Without Ripley's open leadership, information becomes currency. The community develops a gossip-driven power structure instead of an ideological one.
