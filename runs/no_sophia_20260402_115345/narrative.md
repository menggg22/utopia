Everyone is waiting to hear what happens next.

*Says aloud*: ""Friends, let us gather. I want to speak plainly about what we are building here. This is not a refuge from labor — it is a sanctuary *through* labor. We believe that the mind and the hands are not enemies but partners. Every person here — whether philosopher or farmer — will touch both the soil and the page. That is our experiment. That is our covenant. Together, we will prove that a community of equals, sharing work and learning, is not a dream but a living possibility. I will not pretend this will be easy.
