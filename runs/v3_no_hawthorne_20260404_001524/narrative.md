*Inner thought*: My hands are trembling slightly as I gather everyone in the parlor. *Says aloud*: ""My friends—this morning we stand at the threshold of something the world has said is impossible. They said a community cannot unite honest labor with the life of the mind. They said intellectuals cannot work with their hands. They said transcendentalist principles cannot survive contact with mud and necessity. But here we are. Here *we* are.
