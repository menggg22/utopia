# No Hawthorne Run

**Config**: 4 agents (Ripley, Sophia, Dana, Dwight), 30 rounds, seed 42
**Question**: Does the community last longer without the skeptic?

## Final State

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Food | 10 (emergency purchases) |
| Money | $234 (healthiest of all runs) |
| Morale | 0% (since round 11) |
| Departed | None |
| Survived | All 4 |

## Answer: Yes — dramatically better

This is the **best outcome across all experiments**. Nobody leaves. Money ends at $234 (vs $14 baseline). Satisfaction is higher across the board.

## Character Arcs

**George Ripley** — Still declining (72 → 24) but much slower than baseline (65 → 0). Without Hawthorne's skepticism to absorb, Ripley is less burdened. Gave 18 speeches, farmed only 3 times. Still over-talks, under-farms — but is less damaged by it.

**Sophia Ripley** — Thriving. Taught 17/30 rounds, satisfaction 75 → 98. Without the tension Hawthorne creates, she focuses on the school. Her highest satisfaction of any run.

**Charles Dana** — Transformed. Instead of 18 speeches (baseline), he **traded 11 times** and taught 5. Satisfaction 77 → 72 (vs 77 → 0 in baseline). Without Hawthorne absorbing financial anxiety, Dana channeled it into productive action. This is the most striking behavioral shift.

**John Sullivan Dwight** — The happiest version of himself. Organized 10 events, taught 8 times, satisfaction 77 → 100. Without the skeptic's shadow, the cultural heart blooms.

## Key Moments

Only **6 key moments** in 30 rounds (0.2/round) — the lowest of any run. Without Hawthorne's destabilizing presence, there are fewer crises, fewer confrontations, fewer turning points. The community is calmer but perhaps less interesting.

## Key Observations

1. **Removing the skeptic makes everyone happier** — every agent's final satisfaction is higher than baseline. Dwight: 100 vs 7. Dana: 72 vs 0. The skeptic was a psychic drain on the community.

2. **Dana becomes a trader** — the most dramatic behavioral shift. Without Hawthorne occupying the "financial realist" role, Dana fills it productively (trading) rather than destructively (speechifying).

3. **No votes, no proposals** — without the skeptic creating tension, nobody feels the need to propose structural changes. The community drifts contentedly toward the same resource crisis but with less anxiety about it.

4. **Morale still collapses** — 0% from R11. The food crisis is structural (not enough farming) and removing Hawthorne doesn't fix it. But the community copes better emotionally.

5. **Money is healthiest** — $234 vs $14 (baseline). Dana's trading generates more income than speeches.
